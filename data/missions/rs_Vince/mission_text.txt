Location: Southeast of the RegnumDei galaxy, outside a large left radius base
Date: 135.11.07

You sit in front of the porthole and chew protein cubes. You feel that the protein cubes distributed by logistics are getting better and better. Looking at the starships with low-visibility paint lined up outside the window, you feel a little unreal - for three years, you saw the security lights on the original Vallis Lacrimarum working day and night, the factory running all year round, and starships that were basically different from each other constantly coming out of the airport. The young people on Terra Sancta were proud to join the army, and the priests were praying for them. The new space station next to Ardor Ignis has also taken shape. The layout of the top management in the past few decades has shocked you. The long-term vision and the courage to implement it at all costs are something you have never seen before. Since then, you seem to have understood the reasons for these changes.

But your mind can't help but start to diverge. You're thinking that such a force with huge industrial and mobilization capabilities will inevitably come to the forefront through this operation. Regardless of whether you want it or not, you can only wade into the muddy waters of the Sector. You are worried about whether this hard-won peace will be messed up.

The last bit of egg white went into your mouth, and after a couple of rough chews it flowed down your throat. "The taste of this thing has never changed." No matter what, this operation must be carried out, this battle must be won, and the group of terrorists must be killed. As for the rest, there must be a way to the mountain. You stood up and clipped your gun, the cold metal soothing those restless thoughts, "Everyone is on level one combat readiness, combat speed readiness, let's go make those terrorists pay the price."
