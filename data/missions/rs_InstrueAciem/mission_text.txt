Location: RegnumDei System Terra Sancta Surrounding Sector
Date: 134.04.15

You knew that this day would come sooner or later. After all, the Lu Leftists had a good reputation. Although the Knights did not intervene in the star domain dispute, the aftermath of the tsunami could always spread to the entire ocean - but you never expected that this day would come so quickly. It was less than two years after the first contact with them.

Just when you had just finished your duty and wanted to use some alprazolam to save your sleep quality that was destroyed by the long and high-intensity combat readiness duty, the panicked herald disrupted your plan again. "You are a soldier! Even if a supernova explodes in front of you, you can't do this! Copy the rules a few more times after it's over." You were a little annoyed and rushed into the bridge with the herald.

You saw the fleet that covered the sky and the sun, and you seemed to begin to understand the herald. The presence of such a large fleet at such a location means that the defense forces around the jump point have probably been destroyed; after that, there is no longer any obstacle between this group of terrorists and the capital, that beautiful planet, except you.

A hellish mirror image emerges uncontrollably in your mind, and the tsunami-like pressure almost overwhelms you; but you are the commander, and the commander must be strong and must move forward. "The entire fleet is on first-level combat readiness. The fire control room has finished its rotation and the engine room is on alert. The battle speed is ready. Let's block them back!"
