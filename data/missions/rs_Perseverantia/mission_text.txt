Location: Around a jump point in the RegnumDei system
Date: 132.05.21

You have always felt that the so-called "normal patrols" required by the superiors are not necessary - regardless of whether you are just, or appear to be, just a group of poor believers, and this star area is so remote, with no money, food, guns, and ships. At least you can't think of anything in this bad place that is worthy of plundering, unless it is for that precious earth-like planet - but she can't run away; besides, the long-term peace will always make people relax their vigilance.

"Forget it, just do good things with the money." You said to yourself, which was a kind of self-comfort. Staring at the protein block in your hand in a daze, at least you became the commander of such a small fleet and could take care of such a beautiful ship. Thinking of this, the poor-looking protein block in your hand became more delicious.

You raised your hand to put the protein block into your mouth, but the sudden imbalance almost made you choke; faster than your brain's judgment was the herald who stabbed his whole body into the cabin, "Commander, the sensors detected an unknown entity. The first mate ordered the entire fleet to be on level 2 combat readiness, and asked the commander to go to Cambridge." Before your brain could react, your body had adapted to the weightless state of combat readiness, and you began to sneak towards the bridge.

"It's so memorized, I almost choked to death, you idiot." You murmured, then sat in the command position, looking at the marks on the tactical sand table, you issued the first real military order since the establishment of the Knights' armed forces: "The momentum is fierce, the intruder is evil, the entire fleet is at the first level of combat readiness!"
